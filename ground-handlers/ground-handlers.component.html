Ground Handlers


Ground handlers provide various services on the aircraft ramp or apron area. This practice includes marshaling the aircraft to its parking position, connecting ground power and air conditioning units, loading and unloading cargo, fueling the aircraft, and performing safety inspections. We being able to offer a comprehensive range of Ground Operations & Ground Handling related training courses to our students, enhances their ability to be ready for future challenges at airports.
