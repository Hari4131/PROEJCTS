Air Cargo


This course covers the fundamentals of air cargo, from specific terminology in use to key stakeholders in the industry, touching upon digitalization, sustainability, safety, and security. You will get an understanding of why air cargo matters to aviation and to the world, and what global trends and challenges affect the air cargo industry.
